Stefan is a User Experience Research Analyst for Vivint. He is currently working on building out an expansive research program there that can support the needs of our Product Development Teams. His work has provided valuable insights, helping in defining accurate, living personas and use cases.

Prior, Stefan designed for Backcountry.com, Magellan Network, and GeeGuides. He holds a BFA from Winthrop University and was awarded Top 10 Best Software UIs of 2008 by the Nielsen Norman Group.

Stefan believes great design stems from an ability to observe and understand the world around us. It is through empathy that a designer can deliver the kinds of products, experiences and thinking that change and shape this world. He helps deliver the kinds of insights that help designers and businesses bring greater value to the users of their products and services.
