Jaclyn Spangler

HUGGER , FULLSTORY

In Jaclyn’s previous life in the freight forwarding industry, she served as an enterprise Account Manager before moving into a Product Manager position. Saying goodbye to container ships and physical goods, she jumped at the opportunity to join the FullStory team and help build their customer-facing Hugging team using both her product and customer skills. As the world's first Hugger, Jaclyn has been blazing new trails in customer-first product management. Equal parts research, support, %#&-kicking/name-taking and product-shaping action, Jaclyn helps make FullStory a better place for customers every day.
