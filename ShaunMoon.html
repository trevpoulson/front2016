EXPERTICITY

As a senior product manager and designer, Shaun has practiced interdisciplinary leadership, creative collaboration, human-centered design, and product strategy at enterprises in Utah and Pennsylvania.

At Experticity, Shaun serves an audience of 700 consumer brands and nearly a million brand-advocate-users.

As a Senior Product Manager at Instructure, Shaun created Bridge Learning, a new Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) learning management system for organizations that need to quickly create and distribute content to learners using smart phones, tablets or desktop computers. He invented new patterns for managing connections between learners and content and developing goal-based assessments. Bridge was developed using participatory design principles with input and feedback from two dozen organizations.

At Domo, Shaun and his R&D team created a prototype system for collecting and collaboratively making sense of competitive intelligence for sales teams.

Shaun's professional and academic work explores the intersections of design across disciplines related to design theories and methods, computing, physical making and psychology. He has a Masters of Computational Design from Carnegie Mellon University and Bachelors of Architectural Studies from the University of Utah.
