EXPERTICITY

Shaun's life goal is to make using stuff worth it. As a senior product manager at major enterprise software companies, Shaun has developed a broad skill-set of interdisciplinary leadership, creative collaboration, human-centered design, and product strategy.

As a Senior Product Manager at Instructure, Shaun created a new Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) learning platform for organizations that need to quickly create and distribute content to learners using smart phones, tablets or desktop computers. He invented new patterns for developing goal-based assessments and data integration and alidated product concepts with 25 companies across industries.

At Domo, Shaun created an app for collecting and collaboratively making sense of competitive intelligence and worked feverishly to turn copious amounts of data into gold.

Shaun has a Masters in Computational Design from Carnegie Mellon University. He explored the intersections of design across disciplines and contemporary computation through course work in design theories and methods, programming and physical prototyping.
