David Oldham

COO, CO-FOUNDER
OUTRO

Dave is COO and Co-Founder of OUTRO (www.outro.com), a referral automation and personal introductions platform. Dave has over 20 years of experience in product development, sales and marketing, and strategy. Dave has created over 30 different software products - many of which are number 1 in their market segment. Dave loves technology, strategy, competition, social media, advertising, marketing, and brilliant design. He believes in building things that have lasting value, in serving customers and innovating to make their lives better. Dave also loves skiing, snowboarding, racing snowmobiles, wakeboarding, motorcycles, golfing, and most team sports. Dave has an M.B.A. with emphasis in Economics from Columbia University, NYC, and an M.B.A. with emphasis in Entrepreneurship from the University of California, Berkeley.
